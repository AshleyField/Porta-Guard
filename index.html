What is Porta-Guard?

The Porta-Guard mobile security solution offers total automated access control of all pedestrian and vehicle traffic in and out of your site.

The Porta-Guard system can accommodate either one- time or customised continual users and offers both keypad and RFID tag access options.

There is also a mandatory automated induction process to ensure that anyone entering your site fulfils both your company’s as well as legislated requirements.

Fast setup and comprehensive access data logs makes Porta-Guard your complete solution.
